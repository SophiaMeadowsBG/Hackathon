02The insight

Store cold, not electrons.

A material that soaks up a room's heat while it melts — at one constant temperature. Freeze it on cheap night power. Let it melt through the expensive peak.

=
Latent heat of fusion — standard thermodynamics · material datasheets: Rubitherm RT-line paraffins, 10–18 °C grades
